Baseball Cards

These are cards I created as concepts for MLB The Show. Each of these were concepts for the Finest series or the 2020 Draft series. Over the course of my internship, I made roughly 20 concepts, these are just 7 of them. The largest concept was picked to be the concept for the 2020 Finest series and expanded upon after I left my internship. It was important that easch card have the player name, team colors, team logo (clearly shown, not covered), player position, and card type on the card.

Menus

During my time working on MLB The Show, I created a series of menus which players could use to start games, edit their team, and look at player statistics. Throughout this project, it was important to me that players got a sense of motion through the menus. This would help players feel like they were spending less time in menus.
