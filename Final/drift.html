Conversation

The conversation screen allows representatives to chat back and forth with clients. This screen is primarily aimed towards people who are having preliminary talks with people who want to buy the proudct. That’s why key information is all at the top: the customer’s name, their company, and what page they’re looking at is all listed.

When messaging potential clients, the representative’s texts show up as blue while anyone else’s messages show up as gray. This is so the representative can clearly parse the conversation without issue.

Features

These screens allow customers to quickly find what they need to. Often times, representatives have issues finding out how to share a transcript or tag a conversation, this redesign aims to help these representatives more quickly navigate previously complex menus.

During testing, users had a much easier time figuring out where they need to go and how they need to do it.
